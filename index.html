  • Abduction/ kidnapping

    The unauthorised removal of a child from the custody of the child's parent, guardian or caregiver. This includes both abduction by members of the child's family and abduction by strangers.

  • Child labour

    Work that is mentally, physically, socially or morally dangerous and harmful to children and that interferes with their schooling by depriving them of the opportunity to attend school, obliging them to leave school prematurely or requiring them to attempt to combine.

  • Child prostitution

    The use of a child in sexual activities in exchange for something of value, including money, food, housing, drugs, or other commodities such as approval or care.
